My Story Map


by: Amanda Rosenthal


I grew up in Los Angeles California in Sherman Oaks. I have two siblings, two cats, and one adorable goldendoodle named Miles.

I went to a high school called Harvard Westlake, which is located in Studio City.

There I was involved in dance, peer support, was a student ambassador, among much more.

Over the summer, I worked with my best friends Cami and Ava at a day camp called Tocoloma. There, I would manage a group of 10-12 kids and play with them all day.

The majority of my friends lived in the Pacific Palisades, so I would spend most of my weekends there. We would watch the sunset, go hang at the beach, swim, and shop!
